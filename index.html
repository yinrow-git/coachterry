About Coach Terry

Terry Nemoff

A former professional multi-sport athlete turned elite pickleball coach, Terry Nemoff has spent over a decade mastering — and teaching — the fastest-growing sport in America.

Terry's athletic journey began on the golf course at the age of seven, and by twelve he had already earned recognition as a Northern California junior golf champion. Through these early experiences, he developed a strong competitive mindset and a deep understanding of technical sport mechanics. Terry went on to excel across a range of disciplines, including tennis, martial arts, and table tennis. When pickleball rapidly rose in popularity, he quickly recognized its depth and potential. Since then, Terry has established himself as a highly respected coach and competitor within the sport.

Known for his high-energy coaching style, deep tactical knowledge, and genuine passion for helping players improve, Terry works with athletes of all skill levels — from complete beginners to PPA 6.0+ pros looking to sharpen their game.
